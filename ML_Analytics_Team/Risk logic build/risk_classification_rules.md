# Risk Classification Rules

## Risk Levels

| Risk Level | Score Range | Interpretation |
|----------|------------|----------------|
| Low | 0 – 39 | Stable patient |
| Medium | 40 – 69 | Monitor closely |
| High | 70 – 100 | Intervention required |

---

## Classification Notes

- Boundaries are clinically interpretable
- Risk categories are mutually exclusive
- Designed for dashboard consumption
