# Business & Clinical Questions

### Q1. What business value does this provide?
It helps identify high-risk patients early, optimize resource allocation,
and control healthcare costs.

---

### Q2. Can clinicians trust this system?
Yes. Every risk score is explainable with contributing factors clearly shown.

---

### Q3. How does this improve decision-making?
It prioritizes patients based on risk instead of raw counts or intuition.

---

### Q4. Can this scale to large populations?
Yes. The design is batch-based, modular, and built on scalable analytics layers.

---

### Q5. What is the future roadmap?
Add ML models, real-time scoring, and predictive interventions.
