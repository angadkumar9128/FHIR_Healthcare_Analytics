# Feature Weights

| Feature Score | Weight |
|--------------|--------|
| Utilization Score | 0.30 |
| Chronic Burden Score | 0.30 |
| Cost Intensity Score | 0.25 |
| Readmission Score | 0.15 |

---

## Weight Design Rationale

- Utilization and chronic burden drive long-term risk
- Cost reflects financial strain
- Readmission reflects acute instability

Weights are tunable in later stages.
