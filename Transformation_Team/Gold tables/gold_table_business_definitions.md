# Gold Table Business Definitions

## patient_encounter_summary
Provides a consolidated view of patient demographics,
encounter history, and key clinical indicators.

---

## admissions_analytics
Tracks patient admissions, length of stay,
and admission trends over time.

---

## disease_breakdown
Summarizes diagnosis distribution and disease prevalence
across patient populations.

---

## treatment_summary
Aggregates procedures, medications, and treatments
by patient and encounter.

---

## patient_risk_overview
High-level indicators used for downstream risk analysis.
