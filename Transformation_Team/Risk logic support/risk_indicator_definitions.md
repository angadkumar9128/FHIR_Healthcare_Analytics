# Risk Indicator Definitions

## High Utilization Indicator
Patients with frequent encounters
within a defined time window.

---

## Chronic Condition Indicator
Patients with repeated diagnoses
classified as chronic.

---

## Observation Variance Indicator
Patients with observations outside
expected value ranges.

---

## Financial Exposure Indicator
Patients with higher cumulative
insurance claim amounts.

---

## Note
Indicators are analytical signals,
not clinical conclusions.
