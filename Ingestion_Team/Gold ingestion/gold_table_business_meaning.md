# Gold Table Business Meaning

## 1. patient_encounter_summary
### Business Purpose
Provides a unified view of patient demographics along with their
hospital encounters.

### Business Questions Answered
- How many encounters does a patient have?
- When was the patient last admitted?
- What type of encounters are most common?

---

## 2. admissions_analytics
### Business Purpose
Tracks hospital admission trends over time.

### Business Questions Answered
- Daily, monthly, and yearly admission volume
- Peak admission periods
- Admission growth trends

---

## 3. disease_breakdown
### Business Purpose
Analyzes disease prevalence across the patient population.

### Business Questions Answered
- Most common diagnoses
- Disease distribution across patients
- Chronic condition identification
